Description

This report details the development of the Job Search Portal, a comprehensive platform designed to facilitate the job search process for college students while enabling employers to efficiently post job listings and manage applications. The portal draws inspiration from popular job search websites like Naukri.com, Indeed, and LinkedIn, offering a user-friendly interface with intuitive features for an enhanced user experience.

The Job Search Portal aims to streamline the job search process for college students and provide employers with a convenient platform to connect with top talent.
